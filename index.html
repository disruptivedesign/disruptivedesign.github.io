What we do?

Design and develop

Disruptive Design Inc. is a collection of electrical, software and industrial designers who love to build things. We believe a product should be built with a story unbound by common thought. This brings meaning, innovation and intention to the design without the play it safe limitations normally found in a larger design firm. Lets get wild with it.

I hope you enjoy what we build.
