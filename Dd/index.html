What we do?

Design and develop

Disruptive Design Inc. is a collection of electrical, software and industrial designers who love to build cool things. We believe a product should be built with a story unbound by common thought. This brings meaning, innovation and intention to the design without the play it safe limitations normally found in a design firm. Lets get wild with it.

I hope you enjoy what we build.

Purpose Built

Crafted design guided by performance, innovation and focus. Products that do exactly what they're designed to do. Nothing more, nothing less.

Feel Good Products

Our Projects

Delivered by our nifty team passionate about developing disruptive products.

P/squared

We built "Protocol Squared" with simple and effective engineering in mind. Merging live control of industry standard hardware with an intuitive multi-platform user interface.

For Design & Debug
  • I2C & I2S
  • 1.8 to 5 V Logic
  • Visually interactive interface
  • OSX, Windows & Linux

Hacker Blocks

Built for builders by builders. Useful hardware blocks created with intention for quick prototyping, simple electronic design and easy implementation of creative solutions by both beginner and advanced hackers.

For inventors at heart
  • Controllers & Sensors
  • Power & Interface
  • Standardized & easy
  • Arduino compatible

Controller

Controller is an up & coming platform for intuitive control of wireless endpoints from any mobile device. A simple drag and drop app allows users to craft a hands on interface that does exactly what they need for their project. Nothing more, nothing less.

For those who think they're telepathic
  • GPIO, ADC, DAC, I2C & SPI
  • Bluetooth 4.0
  • Drag & drop user interface
  • iOS & Android
